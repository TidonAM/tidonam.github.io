Red Sand Dunes (Saudi Arabia)
Al Qarah Mountain (Saudi Arabia)
Al Hair Lake Park (Saudi Arabia)

Red Sand is an area of red desert sand dunes near Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. The area is a popular destination among Saudis, expatriates, and Filipino workers. Organized tours and other activities are available.

Al-Qarah mountain " Jabal Al-Qarah" also known as " Al-shaba'an mountain " located between At-Tuwaitheer village and Al-Qarah village in Al-ahsa at the Eastern Province, Saudi Arabia. This mountain count as one of the most important natural sights in Al-Ahsa and registered in the UNESCO world heritage centre's list at 2018

Situated around 45 km away from the capital city, Riyadh, the Al Hair Parks and Lakes are a part of the historical Al Hair village in the Wadi Hanifa that houses many old buildings, towers, fortresses, and mosques. Al Hair Parks and Lakes are more commonly known as the Al Hair Lake Park.
